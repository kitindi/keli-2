Welcome to Kibogoji Experiential Learning, Inc (KELI)

We are glad you want to learn more about us! Our mission is to provide state-of-the-art childcare and education for families in Turiani, Morogoro, that recognizes and respects the rights of children, teachers, and families.

KELI is a full-day- year-round program for children aged 3 to 6 years old. We also offer after-school academic enrichment for children of age 7 to 16. Our school has one site at the moment, located in Turiani, Morogoro, near the Turiani Police Post.

The Tanzania Ministry of Education has licensed Kibogoji Nursery school as an early education provider in Tanzania. Our daily operations are run by the Assistant Director and the Site Executive Director.

Our Nursery School

Kibogoji nursery school aims to nurture children’s desire to learn, experiment, play, and gain independence. The curriculum implemented caters to enhancing children’s physical, emotional, social, intellectual, and creative development.

We at Kibogoji are moving into the future to enhance the positive and professional relationships within the Turiani community. Our mission is to cultivate a society that respects and nurtures the learner's rights in an environment that fosters life-long learning through collaboration, compassion, creativity, wonder, and curiosity.
