Welcome to Our Nursery School

Kibogoji nursery school aims to nurture children’s desire to learn, experiment, play, and gain independence. The curriculum implemented caters to enhancing children’s physical, emotional, social, intellectual, and creative development

Our Approach

We at Kibogoji are moving into the future to enhance the positive and professional relationships within the Turiani community. Our mission is to cultivate a society that respects and nurtures the learner's rights in an environment that fosters life-long learning through collaboration, compassion, creativity, wonder, and curiosity. We are committed to a mutually respectful, supportive, and relaxing way of working and being together to create the best possible learning environment for our children and their families.

In our collaboration, we will:

Recognize and nurture the stories, experiences, and learning potential that enrich our image of the child
Provide an environment rich with materials that enable children to express their ideas and explore their experiences
Observe, listen, question, and interact with the children, acting as research
document the children's work as it progresses
Recognize parents and include them as an integral part of the educational process
Provide a comfortable, home-like environment filled with opportunities for communicating and relating

KELI is a STEM learning program.

STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

In Kibogoji's early education programs, our STEM-inspired curriculum integrates the core principles of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics throughout classroom areas and daily routines. It connects academic skills to practical, real-world application, so learning becomes meaningful to children's everyday life.

When you step into our early years' classroom, you will be greeted by engaged and happy children making the most of their time in our well-equipped, bright, and spacious facilities

Why STEM?

Thank you for asking. STEM learning encourages innovations, critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and creativity, resulting in a capable, multi-faceted whole child. Through STEM, children build the skills necessary to navigate the world around them. Making connections between diverse skills and concepts is the way children learn.
