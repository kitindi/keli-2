After School Academic Programs

Learn and get tutoring help in Information Technology,
Mathematics, Science Courses, and other subject areas.

For the after-school academic enrichment program, KELI offers a safe and conducive environment for students to learn and get tutoring help in Information Technology, Mathematics, Science Courses, and other subject areas. This program is run in conjunction with student clubs. Presently, students can enroll in our after-school academic enrichment program anytime throughout the year.

While at the center, students receive customized help in ICT, Maths, English, and other subjects. We also offer pre-form one accelerated program to help standard seven leavers acquaint themselves with the secondary school curriculum.
